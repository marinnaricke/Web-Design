Dog Training Tips!

Follow these 7 easy tips to begin the right training:
  1. Listen to your dog and your dog's needs.
  2. Be generous with your affection and learn to praise and scold with different behaviors.
  3. Learn what your dog likes and reward your dog for good behavior. Dog treats are always the way to go.
  4. Have your dog outside as much as possible.
  5. Be consistent. Tell your dog no at appropriate times and don't confuse your dog.
  6. Have realistic expectations. The dog may not always understand you, so be patient! Give your dog time. Training can be a long process.
  7. Feed your dog the right food. You want your dog to be happy and grow strong.
