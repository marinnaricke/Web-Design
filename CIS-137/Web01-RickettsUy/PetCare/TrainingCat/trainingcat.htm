Cat Training Tips!

Follow these easy tips to starting training right:
  1. Reward behaviors you like.
  2. Use rewards to teach new behavior.
  3. Reward immediately after a certain action occurs. The cat will soon understand what is okay and what is not okay.
  4. Don't force your cat.
  5. Don't turn your cat into a beggar
  6. Never punish your cat. Cat's can obtain a bad attitude and aren't good with connecting the punish with the bad action she did
