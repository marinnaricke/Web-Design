About Loveable Animals

Background Information :

Homeless and abused animals are in need of good homes. More than 5,000 unwanted, abused and stray animals enter the doors of the Loveable Animals Adoption and Rescue Shelter each year. These animals can range from kittens, dogs,even wildlife and exotic animals. Loveable Animals accepts all animals in need.

Many of the animals this shelter cares for were abandoned or given up by owners who can no longer care for them. Sadly some of the animals come to the shelter as victims of abuse or found on the roadside. Loveable Animals is working to improve the lives of Hampstead's homeless animals. Loveable Animals is a non-profit organization and encourages the community to donate so that we can provide the best shelter for these animals. Loveable Animals welcomes volunteer work and any local community help is much appreciated.With your help and the community we can do so much more to care for these animals until they find the right home.

Our goal :

Loveable Animals is aiming to be a true resource for the people and animals of Hampstead. We envision an animal shelter where all animals in good health and temperament find new homes, where they are housed in a low-stress, comfortable environment, where residents can turn in times of personal crisis for help with their pet, where citizens are educated on the proper care of animals, and where abused and neglected animals can receive the love and care they need to recover.

We are very proud of the hard work and dedication our staff puts in to caring for these animals until they are in the best care of new owners. We are also proud of the accomplishments we have made as a organization, but we couldn't have done it without the continuing help of the Hampstead community.With your help, we can continue to achieve so much more than we have already. Our resources are limited and much of our work is done by volunteers. With the support of the Hampstead community, we can find homes for even more animals, improve our operations and the facility to meet the needs of the animals better, and work to prevent animal abuse and abandonment.Every animal should have the right to be happy in a warm and welcoming home.
