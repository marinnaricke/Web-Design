Welcome to the Loveable Animals Adoption and Rescue Shelter,
a non-profit organization created to help the lives of animals. We bring in homeless, neglected, and
unwanted animals in the Hampstead, Md area. Our mission as a shelter is to accept and care
for all animals in need and to promote responsible pet ownership for a more humane community in Hampstead, Md.
